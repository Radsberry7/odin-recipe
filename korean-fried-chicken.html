Korean Fried Chicken

Perfect for cold days. This is a personal recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of chicken breast
  • Cornstarch
  • All purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon of gochujang
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Honey
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Chili flakes
  • Seasame seeds

Directions

Fry the chicken

  1. Cut the chicken into a size that makes it easy to fry.
  2. Prepare the batter - mix in a 1/2 of corn starch and 1/2 a cup of ALl-purpose flour. Mix in ground black pepper, salt, and garlic powder.
  3. Mix the chicken in the batter until it is evenly coated
  4. Heat up oil in a pot or frying pan until it feels hot enough. Fry the chicken until golden brown and set aside.

Gochujang mix

  1. Fry chopped garlic in a bit of oil. Add honey, dark soy sauce, vinegar, tomato sauce and gochujang. Add 250g of hot water into the mix, and cook it a few minutes
  2. Chuck in the chicken and coat the mix until it is even.
  3. Serve with a sprinkle of seasame seeds for aesthetic and crunch.
