Beef Lasagna

Ingredients list

  • 12 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 16 ounce shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat overn to 175 degrees celcius
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 10 minutes or until al dante; drain
  3. Meanwhile, place ground beef, garlic, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until beef is crumbly and evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  4. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.
  5. Lay 4 noodles side by side on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking pan; top with a layer of cottage cheese mixture. Repeat layers wtice more, ending with a layer of sauce; sprinkle mozarella cheese on top. Cover the dish with aluminium foil.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the lasagna is bubbling and the cheese has melted, about 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake until cheese has begun to brown, about 10 more minutes. ALlow to stand at least 10 minutes before serving.
