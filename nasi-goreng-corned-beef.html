Nasi Goreng Corned Beef

Perfect for a lazy cooking day!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cold Bowl of Rice
  • 1/2 tin of corned beef
  • 1 bowl of mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 holland onion
  • 2 bulbs of garlic
  • 'Cukup rasa' powder
  • 3 eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Prepare rice. Chop the onions with the garlic and cili. Grind them. Sautee in medium heat until yellowed.
  2. Mix the corned beef in for a few minutes
  3. Mix in the mixed vegetables, and then the eggs. Stir until the ingredients are all friends. Add a little of salt and 'cukup rasa'
  4. Mix in the cooked rice, until they are together and it looks and smells good.
